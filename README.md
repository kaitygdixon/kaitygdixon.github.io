As a public health professional, I work to understand the system of economic incentives, environmental, genetic, and social determinants of health that lead to our current status quo. And with coursework in epidemiology, biostatistics, behavioral science, health communication, and health politics and policy, I look for improvements and innovations that bring the most good.
